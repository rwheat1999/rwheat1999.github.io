Marching Band Section Leader

For the past 3 years I have been a section leader in my high school's marching band. The director only picks the most skilled and most disciplined marchers and players for this position. Being a section leader is a way to show off my leadership skills and playing ability in the band.

Murphy's Music Award

I have won the Murphy's Music Award for leadership and playing ability my first year playing the Euphonium.

Community Band

Not only am I in my school's concert band, I am also in a community band. I participate in practices in the evening on either Monday's or Tuesday's and then perform at concerts at different venues.
